N-1 vs HA Admission Control

ConstraintHow It Works
N-1Reserve one host's worth of memory
HA AdmissionvSphere setting: reserve X% of cluster

Example: 15 hosts √ó 2 TB each = 30 TB

  • HA 25% reserves 7.5 TB (‚âà N-3 to N-4)
  • N-1 reserves 2 TB (one host)
  • HA wins ‚Äî it's more restrictive
The tool compares both and shows whichever is more restrictive.
